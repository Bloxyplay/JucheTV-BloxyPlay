About This Page

Hey there! thanks for even opening this page — seriously, it means a lot. This whole project is built and maintained by one teenager who just loves coding and sharing cool stuff.

So here's the real situation — the creator is based in Myanmar, where setting up a legit PayPal or any international payment gateway is basically a nightmare (or flat out impossible). So we reached out to some Chinese contacts who were kind enough to let us use their WeChat Pay and Alipay — and that's how these two methods exist! It's a bit indie, a bit scrappy, but it works.

If you're outside China and can't use either method — sharing the project or just vibing with the content is honestly just as valuable! you're awesome either way ~
