About Voice of Korea

Voice of Korea (VOK) is the official international broadcasting service of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea. It broadcasts in multiple languages including Korean, English, Chinese, Japanese, and others. The station provides news, commentary, and cultural programming to international audiences via shortwave, medium wave, and internet streaming.
